Apply pie

Apple pie recipe made to personal preference! I chose this recipe because it I love apple pie and it is versatile.

Ingredients

  • Pie Ingredients
    • 2 cups shortening or lard
    • 1 cup flour
    • 1/2 cup milk or water
  • Pie Filling Ingedients
    • 6 thinly sliced apples
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 2 tbsp cinnamon
    • Dash of nutmeg
  • Egg wash
    • 1 large beaten egg
    • 2 tbsp water

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Prepare crust by mixing shortening, flour, and milk. Set aside.
  3. Mix together the apple slices, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg for the filling. Depending on the apples used or personal taste preference, more or less sugar and cinnamon can be used.
  4. Flour work surface to roll out about half of the pie crust dough made earlier, and fit to the bottom of a pie dish.
  5. Add filling to the pie.
  6. Roll out the remaining dough to complete the crust and remember to cut open a few slits on top to provide vents for the pie
  7. Apply egg wash to the pie crust and bake for about 50 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
